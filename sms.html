Landing Page, Waitlist.

GoneSMS’s design is a derivative of the original branding of Gone. The site continues to suggest to users the outcome of a minimalist and deliberate lifestyle. I implemented the design and copy of the landing page.

GoneSMS was launched with a wait list and referral program. I worked with my colleagues to link up the front end with a custom dashboard to manage signups, referrals, wait list positions, rewards, and item sales.

My dashboard prototype can be found on Github.




Outcome

Both operators and users love the new conversational UX. Connecting the support team with users via text directly increases the number of sales per user and encourages the sale of higher value used goods. We found that Gone's overall average item value increased over 2.2x one month after the SMS launch.

Most interestingly, Gone SMS serves the company’s upcoming milestones by providing an opportunity for hands-on user research and insight into the expectations of younger sellers.
