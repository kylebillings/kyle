I am lucky enough to work with a team of brilliant scientists on this project. After a simple cheek-swab DNA test, our algorithm can create thoroughly personalized nutrition plan based on the particular way one's DNA creates and regulates proteins.

As lead designer, my job is to deliver a hopeful, supportive interface through which users can interact with the foods that may help them to achieve better well-being.
