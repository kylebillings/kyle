My name is Kyle.

I started designing for bands while I was in college, and for the past few years, I've been working with a variety of startups as a product, UX, and visual designer. I have a music business degree from Berklee College of Music and a few years of professional experience in tech.

I like to think about design in terms of how a company's constraints can be arranged to reach the company's goals. I love learning new things (about computers, art, business...) to better understand how these constraints fit together.
